Agriculture Websites

For detailed information on agriculture in India, you can visit the official website of the Department of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, part of the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare (MoA & FW) under the Government of India. This site offers a comprehensive look at various initiatives, schemes, and data related to agriculture in the country, such as crop forecasts, the Minimum Support Price (MSP) system, and area production estimates. You can also find resources on programs like Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana, Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana, and the National Agriculture Market (e-NAM).
