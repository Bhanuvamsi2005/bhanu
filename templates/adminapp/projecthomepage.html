Overview

Rice Plant

Rice is a semi-aquatic plant that grows best in wet places like flood plains, wetlands, ponds, and streams. It can tolerate a wide range of water and soil conditions, from deeply flooded land to dry hilly slopes

Cashew Plant

Cashew is the common name of a tropical evergreen tree Anacardium occidentale, in the family Anacardiaceae. It is native to South America and is the source of the cashew nut and the cashew apple, an accessory fruit.

Maize Plant

Maize, commonly known as corn, is one of the most widely cultivated cereal crops globally. It is a versatile plant grown for its grain, used as food for humans and feed for livestock, as well as for industrial purposes.
